American History

This website is dedicated to learning about the basics of American History and will be a useful resource for all students in the class. I will keep topics updated as we learn about them in class, and will also update grade center as soon as assignments have been graded. Additionally, I will store lesson plans on this site, for use for myself, parents, other teachers, and students. This will help everyone see course objectives, student goals, and classroom procedures. It will also allow for teacher collaboration, and facilitate lesson updates throughout the years. Currently, this is an ongoing process, and is designed to host information I find, and lesson plans I've created, throughout my time at UNH. This will allow me to frequently update, and be able to transition this site to be useful once I am a teacher and have a classroom.
